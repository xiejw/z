Abstractions

There are three abstractions: Policy, Tensor, Func.
  • Policy defines the device of the Tensor, Func, e.g., CPU. It also specifies the lifetime hints, such as Static, Heap, Slab, etc. In future, sharing policy can be put into Policy as well.
  • Tensor is a physical allocated trunk of data, which has few metdata like Dimentions, Type (f32), Location (data pointer), and associated Policy.
  • Func is pure computation, with assocated Policy. It takes Tensors as inputs and produces Tensor outputs.
